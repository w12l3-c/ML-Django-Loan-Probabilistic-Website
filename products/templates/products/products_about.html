About


Current Issue

In the times of the pandemic, countries are overwhelmed by the economic impact. Citizens wanted to borrow a loan but was rejected based on different reason, this can include:

  • Sexism
  • Racism
  • Ableism
  • Classism

    • These problems might not be encountered by the majority of loan applicants. Nonetheless, it will still happen within a community, and often it can and will impact other's life heavily.







    Our goal

    For the problem mentioned above, our website is determined to help those that encounter this kind of problem. Since this is a machine learning calculator, it will unbiasedly determine whether you are a risky borrower or not. Therefore, you can use this as a reference about whether the bank's decision is non discriminant. If not, then you are suggested to look for more organisations to help.

    In the meantime, if you want to or about to apply for a loan and want to know your status, feel free to use this calculator as well. This website is for anyone.



    How you can help

    You can help by support the organizations that help people to tackle these problems. Also, you can promote this website to other people you know that are currently in trouble.

    You can also promote this related issue to increase the awareness of this issue in the community.
